# Localization Strategy

## The Goal
To ensure our "Fairy Tale" metaphors resonate across cultures, prioritizing communities with the highest barriers to medical understanding.

## Phase 1: Two popular Languages 
1. **English:** Baseline.
2. **Spanish:** Primary focus for U.S. clinical accessibility.

## Phase 2: High-Impact Scaling based on locations
* **Mandarin & Vietnamese:** To support large international and immigrant populations.
* **Arabic & Korean:** To address specific regional healthcare needs.

## Linguistic Constraint (Optional)
For every language, the **Fairy Tale Metaphor** must be culturally vetted. 
* *Example:* If a "Dragon" is scary in one culture but a protector in another, the metaphor must swap to ensure the child feels safe.
